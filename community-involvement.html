Community Involvement

A Brief List of Voluntary Commitments

Pembina Trails Public Library

December 2017- Present

The Pembina Trails Public Library host multiple events per year, varying from reading events to children's creativity events. I offer to help and organize the events. I also visit the library frequently and when I have time, I offer to help and volunteer around the library doing odd jobs.

St. Vital, Assiniboine, and Jinnah Park

June 2016- Present

St.Vital, Assiniboine, and Jinnah Park host yearly park cleanup events that I attend and collect trash and disposables to clean the area. I also go voluntarily at random times for the year to clean the public area by myself.

MIA Waverly Mosque

June 2015- Present

My voluntary commitments throughout the mosque are always changing. During the month of Ramadan, I help set up the food, tables and seats. The events are held for people to spend time and eat together as well as feed those who may not have the needed food supply for them to break their fast with or people who are unable to feed themselves.
